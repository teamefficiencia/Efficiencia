ABOUT US

The team started as a student idea at Malla Reddy Engineering College, Hyderabad.Our team is strong enough and incredibly diverse in achieving goals.But the enthusiasm to learn and work is a common thread among the team.We are brought together with our collective passion for a clean future through EV India research.

WHY AI ?

The BMS which we are developing is more effective than the present one .We need to handle constraints while building it and AI is the best effective way to handle constraints.

OUR TEAM

A group of students hailing from different fields, to complete a project successfully we need a interdisciplinary team that connects different domains especially Electrical and CS students.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to tackle the problems arising through battery failures which is leading to forfeiture of EV India .

OUR WORK

With a single mind focus on scalability we are working on a whole host of developing an intelligent BMS which improves the efficiency of EV vehicles.The technological progress we are working on will eliminate the failures of battery and improve the efficiency of the EV India...Know more

OUR VISION

Our vision is to deliver an intelligent Battery Management System which improves the efficiency of EV vehicles.
